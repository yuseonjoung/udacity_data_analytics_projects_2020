Action Report: WeRateDogs Twitter data analysis
¶

Yuseon Joung (08 December, 2020)¶

WeRateDogs is a popular Twitter account that give ratings to a dog picture. The account shares various dog pictures that catch your eyes, make you smile or sometimes even heart-warming.Here in this action report, I share the three insights, which are the results of analyzing 1964 tweets from the account. Moreover, each insight accompanies a brief explanation on which statistical methods were used.

Insight 1:¶

we can expect the average ratings for the numerators for a new dog pictures on WeRateDogs to be in the range of 10.44 and 18.15 with 95% of confidence level.

In order to get this insight, I stimulated a bootstrap sampling 10,000 times, with a sample size of 300.

Insight 2:¶

we observed a positve correlation between the number of favorites and the number of retweets. For every 1 new favorite on WeRateDogs, we can expect the retweet to increase by 2.59.

In order to get the result, a regression model was fit to find the coefficient between the number of favorites and the number of the retweets.

Insight 3:¶

the dog breed with the biggest number of the average of the favorites is Saluki, followed by french_bulldog, afghan_hound, giant_schnauzer, black-and-tan_coonhound, flat-coated_retriever, irish_water_spaniel, standard_poodle, english_springer and leonberg.
